Computer Society of India or CSI is the first and the largest body of computer professionals in India. On March 6, 1965, a few computer professionals had initiated CSI and it has now grown to be a national body that represents computer professionals. It has 71 chapters across India and more than 90,000 members.
KRMU CSI Student Chapter is one of them. It is a community which is developed and embraced by the like minded and passionate students of the K.R. Mangalam University. The objective of this community is to spread awareness and to give opportunities to the students.

OUR MISSION

The mission of KRMU CSI Student Chapter is to facilitate research, Knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the industry and helping them to better integrate into the IT community. To achieve this we provide various hands-on practical workshops,events,seminars and projects to all our members so that they can achieve and learn the most out of it.
